About.

    @bmlindor | @bmlindor | @bmlindor

I'm currently a NSF Graduate Research Fellow studying Astronomy at the UW. In my free time, I enjoy stories – most often those told via books and video games. I have an eclectic music taste and sing almost all the time.

I've always been interested in science and discovery. In 2018, I received a B.A. in Astrophysical Sciences and a certificate in Planets and Life from Princeton University.

I was born in Port-au-Prince, Haïti and moved to the U.S. state of New Jersey as a child. Now, I live in the great PNW, where I enjoy the sunshine when I can get it.

I'm a Sagittarius Sun and Moon, with a rising Taurus. Throughout my experiences, I have come to value community, friendship, and care in all aspects of my life.
