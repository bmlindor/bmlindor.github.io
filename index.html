Intro

Education:

Ph.D. in Astronomy, University of Washington (expected 2024)

B.A. in Astrophysical Sciences, Princeton University (2018)

Research Interests:

Generally interested in exoplanets, galactic astronomy, and astro-informatics.

Currently working on a project at the University of Washington (UW) with Eric Agol to derive and constrain planetary system parameters by modeling transit timing variations.

About Me:

Favorite things include singing, reading, video games, friendship, and (not) thinking about the vast emptiness of space.

I am dedicaded to equity, diversity, and inclusion; see Community for the ways that I have found to make broader impacts.
